Kirkland Signature Colombian Coffee Review

Dark Roast • 3 lbs • Costco Exclusive

★★★★☆ (4/5)

Overview

Kirkland Signature Colombian Coffee is a well-balanced dark roast that delivers a bold yet smooth flavor. Packaged in a generous 3-pound bag, it’s perfect for households that go through coffee quickly. True to its Colombian origin, the beans have a rich aroma and slightly nutty undertones.

Tasting Notes

  • Strong aroma with earthy undertones
  • Smooth, bold flavor without bitterness
  • Hints of cocoa and toasted nuts

Pros & Cons

Pros
  • Affordable price for bulk size
  • Rich and consistent flavor
  • Perfect for drip coffee makers & French press
Cons
  • Only available at Costco
  • Not ideal for espresso machines
  • Large bag may lose freshness if not stored well

Final Verdict

If you’re looking for a daily coffee that delivers bold Colombian flavor without breaking the bank, Kirkland Signature Colombian Coffee is a fantastic choice. It’s not the most exotic coffee out there, but it’s reliable, rich, and an excellent value.
